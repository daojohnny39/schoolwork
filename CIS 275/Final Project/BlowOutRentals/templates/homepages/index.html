Our Rentals

Blowout Rentals is the perfect place for you to rent your favorite instrument. We offer both used and new instruments in order to give customers both better selection and pricing.

About Us

Founded in 2018, Blowout Rentals began its story in the basement of a one-story home in the suberbs of Provo, Utah. Due to the increasing popularity of music over the last year, Blowout Rentals has grown tremendously and now has several shops across Utah Valley. We offer a wide selection of instrument rentals, ranging from flutes to saxophones and offer various insurance and protection packages to ensure a positive rental experience.
